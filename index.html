About Lampang Province

Lampang is a unique province located in northern Thailand. It has a long and rich history and preserves its beautiful Lanna culture very well.

Lampang is the only city in Thailand that still uses horse carriages as a means of transportation. It is also an important center for Thai elephant conservation.

Popular Tourist Attractions

Wat Phra That Lampang Luang

Wat Phra That Lampang Luang is an important historical and cultural temple. It features a beautiful Lanna-style chedi and is a symbol of Lampang Province.
